Implementation Instructions

To use this race number design in your project:

  • Create a container div with class race-number-container
  • Inside, add three divs:
    • side-panel (left white border)
    • center-panel (main content area)
    • side-panel (right white border)
  • Inside the center-panel, add:
    • logo-area (top white section for the logo)
    • number-area (gold section for the rider number)
  • To use your actual logo, replace the placeholder with:
